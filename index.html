Before you flash

Browser: desktop Chrome or Edge (Safari and Firefox don't support WebSerial yet).

Cable: a data USB-C cable, not charge-only.

Device: plug the AirLock straight into your computer. The board enters bootloader mode automatically when needed.

What gets flashed

The firmware is updated while your existing settings (modes, calibration, Wi-Fi) are preserved. On first install you'll be asked whether to erase settings — choose no to keep them, or yes for a clean start.

If the install button doesn't appear, your browser doesn't support WebSerial. Use Chrome or Edge on a laptop / desktop. Phones and tablets can't flash directly.
